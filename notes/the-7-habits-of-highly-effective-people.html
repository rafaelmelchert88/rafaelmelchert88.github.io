The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People - Notes

The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People

Author: Stephen R. Covey

Notes:

  • Independece (Self-Mastery)
    • Be proactive

      • Decisions, effectiveness. Choices and consequences

    • Begin with the end in mind

      • Personal values, life goals. Attitudes and relationships

    • Put first things first

      • Prioritize, plan and execute weekly tasks. Evaluate your efforts.

  • Interdependence (Working with others)
    • Think win-win

      • Mutually beneficial solutions.

    • Seek first to understand, then to be understood

      • Empathy.

    • Synergize

      • Combine the strenghts

  • Continual improvement
    • Sharpen the saw

      • Growth.
      Balance and renew your resources, energy and health.
      Effective and sustainable lifestyle.
      Physical execise, meditation and good reading (mental renewal).
      Service to society (spiritual renewal).

    • Upward Spiral

      • Learn, commit, do.
